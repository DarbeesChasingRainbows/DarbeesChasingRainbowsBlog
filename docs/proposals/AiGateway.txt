1. Executive Summary & Unified Vision
The Darbee Family Sovereign AI Platform is a single, local-first, Bible-aligned, multi-generational AI system that protects your children while capturing and multiplying your family’s lived wisdom.
It combines two tightly integrated subsystems:

KidSafe AI Gateway — Deterministic safety layer, age-appropriate education, voice interaction, vision moderation, and parent oversight for your four children (ages 4, 7, 10, 12).
DAIS (Darbees Architectural Intelligence System) — Agentic content intelligence layer that turns your Obsidian vault, build photos, theology notes, and farm decisions into published Astro content and a living Family Legacy Graph.

Core Commitments

Everything that touches your children passes through deterministic, layered safety (never delegated to LLMs).
Everything that captures family story, builds, and principles flows through DAIS with full provenance and versioning.
Both systems share one ArangoDB knowledge graph, one LM Studio model server, one .NET 9 Semantic Kernel runtime, and one Podman-based deployment on Bazzite DX.
The platform is designed for RV life (on-demand/scheduled operation) and grows with your children from preschool to adulthood.
All inference, storage, and orchestration is 100% local and sovereign.

Primary Goals

Protect children with biblical, stewardship-centered guardrails.
Automatically adjust content complexity for each child.
Capture and publish regenerative farming + theology content while maintaining family voice.
Build a queryable, versioned, multi-generational Family Legacy Graph.
Give parents (you and your wife) real-time visibility + simple approval workflows.
Export family stories as books, audio, and time-capsule PDFs for your children’s future.

5.2 DAIS Content Intelligence Layer

ObsidianPlugin: Reads/writes Markdown, manages YAML frontmatter, detects AI Classify toggle on images
AssetPlugin: Local LLaVA entity extraction + Cloudflare Images “washing” for permanent hosting
GEOPlugin: LM Studio-generated citable GEO/SEO metadata
PublishPlugin: Emits clean .mdx files to Astro src/content folder
LegacyGraphPlugin: Creates dual nodes (DAIS publishable + KidSafe safe-retrieval) for every marked “Lived Experience” item

5.3 Shared Agent Orchestration
Both subsystems use the same Semantic Kernel instance with Automatic Function Calling.
The orchestrator respects tenant isolation and routes through the shared Safety layer when content will be consumed by children.
