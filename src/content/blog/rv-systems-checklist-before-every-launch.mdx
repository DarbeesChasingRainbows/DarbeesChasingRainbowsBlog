## The list

It lives on a laminated card by the door. We've thrown out three earlier versions; this one has survived two years.

1. Slides in (and the dog out from under them).
2. Antenna down.
3. Steps up.
4. Awning latched.
5. Fridge to LP off / electric only.
6. Water pump off, lines drained if it'll freeze.
7. Cabinets latched (the upper one always wins).
8. Loose items on the counter — gone.
9. Hitch pin and safety chains (two checks, two people).
10. Brake controller responding.
11. Tire pressures green.
12. Coffee mug in the cup holder — *not on the counter.*

<Callout title="The two-person rule">
Anything that ends a trip badly — hitch, chains, brake controller — gets two sets of eyes. No exceptions, no shortcuts.
</Callout>

## Why a written list still wins

We've been doing this for years. We still miss things from memory. The card is faster than the muscle memory and never has a bad morning.
