## Hello from the road

We're the Darbees, and this is our little corner of the internet. We're documenting the road as we go — the RV projects, field notes, family lessons, useful builds, and the dream of someday building **Kingdom Farm**.

This blog is part journal, part archive, and part open notebook. If any of it helps another family along the way, that's a bonus.

## What you'll find here

We organize our writing into three broad lanes:

- **Blog** — family updates, faith reflections, recipes, gear thoughts, and the everyday stuff
- **Field Notes** — hikes, nature studies, historic sites, and homeschool field trips
- **Projects** — DIY builds, RV automation, schematics, and code we've shipped

<Callout type="faith" title="Why we're here">
  We believe ordinary days are worth paying attention to. Writing them down keeps us honest, slows
  us down, and reminds us that small things matter.
</Callout>

## Where we're headed

Long-term, we're working toward [Kingdom Farm](/kingdom-farm) — a family homestead with a Christmas tree farm, an RV park, hospitality, and room for the people we love. It's a long road. We'll show our work as we go.

## Stick around

If any of this resonates, [subscribe to the newsletter](/#newsletter), [grab the RSS feed](/rss.xml), or just bookmark the [field notes index](/field-notes). We're glad you're here.
