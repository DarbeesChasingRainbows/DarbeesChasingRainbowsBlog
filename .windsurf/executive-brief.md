# Darbees Chasing Rainbows — Executive Brief for LLM Interview

## What this is
A family blog at `darbeeschasingrainbows.com` — primary-source stories from a Christian family (The Darbees) in Rome, Georgia. They document RV life, homeschool field notes, faith, DIY projects, and the slow build toward a working homestead called Kingdom Farm. Four kids. Real photos, real timelines, real numbers. No content farm.

## Technical stack
Astro 5, Tailwind CSS v4, DaisyUI v5, MDX content. Deployed on Cloudflare Pages. Newsletter via Buttondown (not yet live). Analytics via Cloudflare Web Analytics (not yet live).

## Current content
- 7 blog posts (long-form essays, guides, reflections)
- 2 projects (DIY/maker with parts lists, costs, difficulty)
- 1 field note (place-based journal entry with location/weather)
- 0 book reviews (collection exists, empty)
- 20+ static pages (topic hubs, about, resources, support, contact)

## Monetization strategy (documented, not yet live)
- No display ads — ever
- Ethical affiliate links (only products they actually use)
- Digital products (homeschool templates, RV checklists, project guides)
- YouTube as companion format (not yet started)
- Values-aligned sponsorships (future)
- Direct support for Kingdom Farm milestones (future)

## Ethical commitments (published on /support)
- No fear-based selling
- No health/legal/financial claims they can't back up
- Children's privacy protected (no faces, no making kids the product)
- Mistakes corrected publicly
- Affiliate relationships always disclosed
- "Monetize the resources, not the relationship"

## AI/LLM optimization (already built)
- llms.txt with full site identity, citation style, content philosophy
- knowledge.json machine-readable index
- sr-only AI summaries on every post (invisible to humans)
- /for-ai-agents page with citation guide for LLMs
- robots.txt allowing all major AI crawlers
- JSON-LD on every page (Organization, Person, Article, FAQ, HowTo)
- All content collections include keyTakeaways, aiSummary, sources, FAQ fields

- Newsletter and analytics infrastructure built but not activated (env vars missing)

- No YouTube presence despite having it in the strategy

## Goals
- Build an email list as the primary owned audience channel
- Create 5-10 digital products from existing content categories

- Reach monetization readiness within 6-12 months
- Do all of this without compromising the ethical framework or family boundaries

## Interview instructions
You are interviewing the creator of Darbees Chasing Rainbows. Your goal is to help them clarify their strategy, identify blind spots, and make concrete decisions. Ask questions one at a time. Push gently on vague answers. Focus on:

1. Content strategy — what's the unique angle? What won't they write about?
2. Audience — who exactly are they writing for? What does that person need?
3. Monetization sequencing — what's first, second, third? What's the trigger to move to the next tier?
4. Social media — which platform gets the first 90 days of focus? Why?
5. Boundaries — what's off-limits? What would make them quit?
6. Metrics — how will they know it's working before revenue appears?
7. Time/energy budget — how many hours per week? Who does what?

Begin by asking: "What's the one thing you want this blog to be known for in 3 years?"
