## Q1: Content Strategy

**Question:** What's the one thing you want this blog to be known for in 3 years?

**Answer:** Faithful living in times of uncertainty, moving in faith, stewardship of our children, and building for God's kingdom

---

## Q2: Content Strategy (follow-up)

**Question:** How does this translate to your actual content — what topics or angles will you embrace to live out that vision, and what will you deliberately NOT write about (even if it might get traffic)?

**Answer:** I need ideas of what to write about, I have a lot of topics, but I'm not sure where to focus all of my time, but I won't write anything that is exploiting my children or compromising my faith or convictions

**Clarification received:** The blog should be about ONE thing: "Building a real, faith-centered life in a modern world that doesn't support it."

**Core identity:** A family building a more intentional, faith-rooted life through real constraints, honest experience, and the long road toward something lasting.

**Pillars (support the story, not the main thing):**
- RV Life → constraint + tension
- Homeschool → how you raise your kids
- Field Notes → how you observe the world
- Projects/Automation → how you solve problems
- Books → how you think and discern
- Kingdom Farm → where it's all going

**Content test:** "Does this help someone understand how to live more intentionally in real constraints?"

**Audience refinement:** People who feel something is off about modern life, want something more intentional, but don't know how to get there and don't trust polished influencers.

---

## Q3: Audience

**Question:** Who exactly is your ideal reader? What season of life are they in, and what specific problem or need are you solving for them?

**Answer:** (Based on brand requirements document)

**Ideal reader includes:**
- Families already full-time RVing
- Families considering full-time RV life
- Homeschool families
- Christian families
- Future homesteaders
- Parents interested in food independence
- Preparedness-minded families
- Readers wanting inspiration to live differently

**NOT for:**
- Glam RV lifestyle content seekers
- Secular-only homeschool resource seekers
- Unschooling advocates
- Face-forward family vlogging fans
- Fear-based prepper content consumers
- Generic affiliate gear list seekers
- Aesthetic-only homesteading enthusiasts
- Passive-income fantasy seekers

**Content serves:** Both people currently living the lifestyle and those considering it, with honest enough content to help people make wise decisions without overselling RV life, homeschool, or farm dreams.
