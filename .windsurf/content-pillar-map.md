# Darbees Chasing Rainbows — Content Pillar Map

## Editorial spine

Darbees Chasing Rainbows is about a Christian family building a more intentional, faith-rooted life through real constraints, honest experience, and the long road toward Kingdom Farm.

The categories are not the identity. They are evidence of the deeper story.

## Content filter

Before writing anything, ask:

> Does this help someone understand how to live more intentionally and faithfully in real constraints?

If yes, it probably fits. If no, it is probably noise.

## First 90-day focus

### Primary lanes

1. **Homeschool under constraints**
   - Best mix of usefulness, trust, lead magnets, and recurring post ideas.
2. **RV life as constraint and tension**
   - Differentiates the story and creates honest, practical posts.
3. **Field Notes / location-based learning**
   - Bridges homeschool, travel, photography, resources, and social media.

### Supporting lanes

1. **Bookshelf / discernment**
   - Low-friction recurring trust builder.
2. **Kingdom Farm**
   - Long-term narrative arc; update honestly without overpromising.

### Hold lighter for now

- **Projects & useful builds** should publish when there is a real solved problem, not on a forced schedule.
- **General faith reflections** should support lived stories rather than become abstract devotional content.

## First lead magnet recommendation

Start with the **Field Notes Starter Pack**.

Why:

- It already matches an existing resource direction.
- It bridges homeschool, travel, field notes, books, and family formation.
- It is easy to test publicly without needing paid-product polish.
- It can be expanded into a paid complete pack later.

Suggested contents:

- One-page field note sheet
- Family observation prompt sheet
- Read-before-you-go planning page
- Nature/history vocabulary list
- Parent guide: how to use field notes without over-schooling the outing

## Publishing rhythm recommendation

For the next 90 days:

- 2 blog posts per month
- 1 field note per month
- 1 book review per month
- 1 resource improvement per month
- Social media repurposing from every post, not separate original work

## Social media translation

Every post should become at least one of:

- Instagram carousel
- Instagram caption essay
- YouTube Shorts / Reel voiceover over B-roll
- Pinterest pin to the blog/resource
- Newsletter section

## What not to chase yet

- Daily posting
- Face-to-camera content as a requirement
- Generic affiliate roundup posts
- Membership
- Sponsorships
- Kingdom Farm fundraising before clearer milestones
